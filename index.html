02 — Enterprise field-ops

AttendX

GPS-gated attendance for shift & field teams.

Employees clock in and out against geofenced job sites; an offline queue captures punches with no signal and syncs them in order on reconnect. Built for the messy real world — mock-location detection, clock-drift warnings, multi-session shifts, overnight shifts and biometric login. A Django admin portal handles orgs, projects, shifts, assignments and attendance reporting.

  • Haversine geofencing
  • Offline sync queue
  • Biometric login
  • Multi-session shifts
  • Overnight shifts
  • JWT auto-refresh
